The ingredients needed to make Motton peas keema:
  1. Get 1/2 kg motton keema
  2. You need 1 cup peas
  3. Get 3/4 tbsp salt
  4. You need 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Provide 1 tsp zeera
  7. You need 1 tsp crush Coriander
  8. Take 2 green chilli and Coriander
  9. Take 1 tsp ginger julienne
  10. Provide 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  11. Use 2 tomato cubes
  12. Use 1 large onion slice
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  14. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  15. Get Green/ red chilli, green Coriander and ginger julienne for garnishing
  16. You need 1 tbsp desi ghee
  17. Use 1/2 cup oil
Instructions to make Motton peas keema:
  1. Wash and clean the motton keema and set aside.
  2. Now heat the pan add oil and onion slices fry it then add ginger garlic paste then add motton keema stir it well.
  3. Next add all the spices and mix the motton keema until it's colour changes than add lit bit water and cook it well.
  4. When the colour becomes change then add green peas and stir it then add 1 glass of water you can add jinger julienne, green chilli and Coriander and cook it until it becomes tender completely, then add desi ghee.
  5. A yummy motton peas keema is ready to serve, eat with naan and roti.
