The ingredients needed to prepare Stuffed Chicken breast wrapped in Bacon:
  1. Use 2 chicken breasts
  2. Provide Handfull of diced mixed peppers
  3. You need 1 piece Cottage cheese
  4. Provide 100 ml basil pesto (refer to my basil pesto recipe)
  5. Get 2 streak bacons
  6. Provide Toothpicks
  7. Take 1 tablespoon paprika
  8. Get 1 tablespoon salt
  9. Provide 1 tablespoon black pepper
  10. Get 1 tablespoon chicken spice
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon olive oil
Instructions to make Stuffed Chicken breast wrapped in Bacon:
  1. Dice peppers
  2. Season the breast with salt, paprika, black pepper & chicken spice. Cut the breasts in the middle to stuff them..
  3. Stuff with diced peppers
  4. Pour the basil pesto on top of peppers
  5. Sprinkle feta on top of basil pesto
  6. Close chicken then Wrap bacon around it securing with toothpicks
  7. Bake in oven at 200°C for 20 minutes or till chicken is cooked
  8. Remove toothpicks & serve with vegies or salad of your choice.
