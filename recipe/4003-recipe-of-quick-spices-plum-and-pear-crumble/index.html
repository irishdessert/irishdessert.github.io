  5. Provide 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

  8. Prepare 120 g butter, cut into small cubes
  9. Provide 1 cup plain flour
  10. You need 1 tsp ground cinnamon

  12. Take Pinch salt
  13. Take 2-3 tbsp decicated coconut for sprinkle on top (optional)
Instructions to make Spices Plum and pear crumble üçêüçë:
  1. Peeled and cut up your pears and cut your plum in quarters.
  2. Add your fruits in a saucepan and add some sugar. Turn heat on low heat and let it cook for 10-15 min or until they soft. Now time to make crumble while waiting for fruits to cook.
  3. To make crumble, mix butter and flour together and use your hand to crumble and mix them together. Dont over do it until they form to dough this is essential as you want your topping to be crumbly.

  5. Once your fruits cooked, place them on a baking tray (I use pie dish)
  6. Add your crumble on top, I like to sprinkle some decicated coconut on top too. I think it goes well with ground cinnamon and ginger.
