The ingredients needed to prepare Eggless Fresh Coconut Pecan Cookies:
  1. You need 2 cup shredded coconut (Fresh)
  2. You need 1/2 cup Pecan
  3. You need 1 cup all purpose flour
  4. You need 200 grams sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  6. Use 1/4 cup milk
  7. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Use 1/4 teaspoon salt
Instructions to make Eggless Fresh Coconut Pecan Cookies:
  1. In a dry bowl, beat butter and sugar together until it forms a creamy mixture. To it add sifted all purpose flour,baking soda,salt and shredded coconut and mix until it forms a crumbly mixture. To it sprinkle milk little by little to a form a cookie dough. Roll into a log and add some more coconut and cling wrap and refrigerate for an hour. Then slice it as shown.
  2. In a baking tray,place each slice gently and press pecan on it.
  3. Preheat oven to 180 degree Celsius and bake it for 20 minutes. Cool it on a cooling rack and store it in an airtight container. Crunchy outside and soft inside Cookie is ready. Eggless Fresh Coconut Pecan Cookie is ready to consume with a cup of Coffee and Tea.

Cookie dough and can be kept frozen for up to a month. These eggless coconut cookies are soft and chewy; it is hard to stop with just one cookie, so addictive. It is easy to make and hardly one can go wrong with this recipe. I love to have these cookies with cup of tea; it is not too sweet and is full of coconut flavour. And also I have made it little healthier by reducing amount of butter.
