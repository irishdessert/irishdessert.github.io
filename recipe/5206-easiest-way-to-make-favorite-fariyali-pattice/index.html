The ingredients needed to cook Fariyali Pattice:
  1. Get For stuffing
  2. Use 1 cup Fresh grated coconut (peel brown skin too)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) grounded
  4. Provide 2 Green Chillies
  5. Take 1/2 cup chopped cashew
  6. You need 1/4 cup raisins
  7. Provide 1 tsp Sugar
  8. Use 1 tsp rock salt (sendha namak)
  9. Provide 1 tsp lemon juice
  10. Take for pattice
  11. Prepare 6 boiled and mashed potatoes
  12. You need 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  13. Take to taste Rock Salt
  14. Prepare 3 tsp rajgira aata(flour) or arrowroot powder
  15. Get As needed Oil for frying
Steps to make Fariyali Pattice:
  1. In a mixing bowl, add grated coconut, grounded peanut powder,cashew and raisins and all stuffing mix well and make small balls of this.
  2. Take boiled potatoes and mashed them. - Add 3 tbsp arrowroot flour or rajgira aata,coriander leaves and salt to taste.Mix well. Make 15 medium balls from this.
  3. Gently flatten the potato balls with the help of your palm. Place a ball of stuffing and now cover from all sides ensuring there are no cracks and form them into a round shape. - Just repeat the same with the rest of balls. - Place oil in a deep frying pan. Once oil is hot, fry the pattice balls until golden brown. Place them over absorbent napkin to move the extra oil. - Farali Pattice can be served with dhaniya pudina chutney.enjoy them hot.

Farali Pattice Recipe is a healthy fasting food of the Gujarati's.&nbsp; Faral means 'fast' and these patties are specially made for those who are fasting. The patties are made with an outer covering of mashed potatoes and filled inside with spiced dry fruits mixture, coconut and peanuts. The patties are traditionally fried but as a lover of healthy non fried food I am sharing the. That's the reason why the name of this recipe is Faraali Pattice Recipe.
