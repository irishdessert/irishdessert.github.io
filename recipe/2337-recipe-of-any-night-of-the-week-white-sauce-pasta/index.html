The ingredients needed to cook White sauce pasta:
  1. Provide For boiling pasta
  2. Get 6 cup water
  3. Provide 1 tsp salt
  4. Get 2 cup pasta
  5. You need For sauteeing vegetables
  6. Provide 2 tsp oil
  7. You need 3 garlic cloves finely chopped
  8. You need 1/2 onion chopped
  9. Take 1/2 capsicum chopped
  10. Provide 1/2 tsp black bpeeper crushed
  11. You need To taste salt
  12. You need for white sauce
  13. Use 2 tsp butter
  14. Prepare 3 tsp plain flour
  15. Use 2 cup milk
  16. Provide 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes
  17. Prepare to taste salt
  18. Use As needed Cheese for garnishing
Steps to make White sauce pasta:
  1. Firstly, a large kadai boil 6 cup water and 1 tsp salt, - once the water comes to a boil, add 2 cup pasta.
  2. boil for 7 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked al dente, drain off the pasta and keep aside.
  3. In a frying pan, heat 2 tbsp oil and saute 3 clove garlic Now add ½ onion, ½ capsicum
  4. Saute for a minute or until vegetables shrink slightly yet remain crunchy, further, add ½ tsp pepper and ½ tsp salt.
  5. Saute well until the spices are well combined and keep aside now add 1 cup milk and mix well. mix until the sauce thickens without forming any lumps further, add 1 more cup of milk and continue to stir continuously, cook until the sauce turns silk smooth and creamy texture.
  6. Now add ½ tsp pepper, ½ tsp chilli flakes, and ½ tsp salt, mix well making sure everything is well combined. add in sauted vegetables and combine well.
  7. Now add in boiled pasta and mix gently, add 2 tbsp of boiled pasta water if the sauce thickens, mix gently making sure everything is well combined.
  8. Finally, just before serving white sauce pasta, garnish with grated cheese.

Preparation of White Sauce pasta (without milk): Take a wok and heat the oil in it. Once the oil gets heated, add the chopped up onions. Sauté the onions until slightly brown, don't overcook. Put the saucepan on the hob over a medium heat and whisk continuously until the butter melts and the sauce is thickened and smooth. Season with salt and pepper then use in your recipes.
