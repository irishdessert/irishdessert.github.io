The ingredients needed to cook Spicy Chicken Gizzards:
  1. Provide 500 g cleaned chicken gizzards
  2. Provide 1 chopped onion
  3. Take 1 red pepper, sliced into strips
  4. Prepare 1 yellow pepper sliced into strips
  5. Prepare 1 green pepper sliced into strips
  6. Prepare Cooking Oil
  7. Prepare 2 tspns chicken spice
  8. Get 2 tspns BBQ spice
  9. Get 2 tspns ginger & garlic
  10. You need 2 tspns Paprika
  11. Prepare 2 chopped green chillies
Instructions to make Spicy Chicken Gizzards:
  1. Pre-heat oil and add onoins
  2. Add ginger & garlic and chopped chillies
  3. Add cleaned gizzards and cook for 20mins
  4. Add all peppers and cook for another 15mins
  5. After that it should be ready to serve
