The ingredients needed to cook Chicken Parmesan Sliders:
  1. Prepare 1 pound thin sliced chicken
  2. You need 1/2 pack (6) of dinner rolls (or Hawaiian rolls)
  3. Get 1/4 cup marinara sauce
  4. Prepare 1 cup (4 ounces) sliced or grated mozzarella cheese
  5. Get 1/8 cup fresh basil
  6. You need topping
  7. Prepare 4 tablespoons butter
  8. Use 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  9. Use 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  10. Get 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan
Steps to make Chicken Parmesan Sliders:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Season chicken if you desire (i used an Italian seasoning)
  3. Cook chicken until done. Set aside.
  4. Melt butter, mix other topping indigents into the butter
  5. These are the type of rolls you are looking for, cooked, attached. Usually come in a 12 pack. I am using half (6) in this recipe.
  6. Cut sheet of rolls in half lengthwise, keep them attached. I start them like this.
  7. Then move to this way.
  8. Lay the bottom half in your baking pan
  9. Make your marinara sauce (wink wink)
  10. Put a light coat of sauce on the bottom rolls.
  11. De-stem your basil
  12. Chop your basil
  13. Layout your chicken on the bottom
  14. Cover the chicken with the marinara sauce
  15. Put your mozzarella cheese on next
  16. Spread the basil on
  17. Place top half of the rolls, give it a little, light press, just to "set" it
  18. Stir your topping one last time. Brush it over the top of the rolls.
  19. Place in the oven, cook 20 minutes.
  20. Remove, let sit a couple minutes.
  21. Cut to separate the sliders.
  22. Serve

Place cooked chicken tenders on the bottom buns and then spoon the sauce over the chicken. Top toasted bottom buns with the cooked chicken mixture and the mozzarella cheese. Close the sandwich with the top halves of the. Chicken Parmesan Sliders are sweet dinner rolls toasted with garlic butter and topped with Parmesan cheese. Slices of pan-fried chicken, marinara sauce and piles of mozzarella cheese top this picture perfect slider.
