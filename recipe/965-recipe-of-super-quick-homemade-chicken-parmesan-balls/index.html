The ingredients needed to prepare Chicken-parmesan balls:
  1. Provide 600 g chicken breast
  2. Provide 1 cup parsley
  3. Use 1 cup cilantro
  4. Use 1 cup parmesan
  5. Prepare 2 tbls garlic paste
  6. Use 1 tbls dijon mustard
  7. Use 2 stalks green onions
  8. Use 1 Chilli pepper
  9. You need 2 eggs
  10. Take to taste Salt
  11. Use 1/4 cup vegetable oil
Instructions to make Chicken-parmesan balls:
  1. In a food processor, mix in all the ingredients as listed above (use 1 egg here) and process until the ingredients are processed and mixed well.
  2. Scoop a tbl of the mixture and form into balls. Arrange the koftas on a plate.
  3. Allow the chicken-parmesan balls to rest in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes
  4. In a bowl, beat 1 egg and keep aside to dip the chicken balls before frying
  5. Over high heat, in a frying pan, add 1/4 cup of cooking oil and shallow fry the balls until cooked through
  6. Serve with pita bread and a yoghurt mint sauce or Tahini
