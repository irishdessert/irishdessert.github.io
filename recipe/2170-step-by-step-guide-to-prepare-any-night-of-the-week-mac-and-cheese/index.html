The ingredients needed to cook Mac and Cheese:
  1. Get 500 g Macaroni
  2. Get 1 Red pepper
  3. You need 1 Green pepper
  4. Prepare Olive oil to fry
  5. Take 3 Chicken fillets
  6. Get 125 ml milk
  7. Provide 50 g three cheese
  8. Take 125 g grates cheddar cheese
  9. Take To taste paprika
  10. Get To taste ground pepper
  11. Take To taste salt
  12. Take To taste mixed herbs
Steps to make Mac and Cheese:
  1. Follow the pack of macaroni and cook all dente.
  2. Cut the chicken into strip and in a pot then pour the olive oil and start to fry the chicken until it's golden brown.
  3. Chop your peppers and pour in a chicken pot as it has turn golden brown.
  4. Stir and start to season with spices to taste. Then once it's cooked then mix macaroni with chicken stir until it's mixed properly
  5. In a cup pour the satchet of three cheese and add milk stir and pour it over the mac and chicken
  6. Stir and mix and let it thick, and low your heat.
  7. Pour that grated cheese to make it more cheese, delicious and tasty
  8. Enjoy me and my grandkids we will enjoy with juice

Meanwhile, melt the butter in a saucepan. Put macaroni in large casserole or baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Melt remaining butter in a frying pan over medium heat. Spread over the macaroni to cover. I made this for my toddler and he loves it!
