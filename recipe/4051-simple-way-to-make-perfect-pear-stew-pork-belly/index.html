The ingredients needed to cook Pear stew pork belly:
  1. Provide Salt
  2. You need Garlic
  3. Get Pork belly (any meat can do,chicken too)
  4. You need Tomato
  5. Take Pear
  6. Get Dry goji berries
  7. Prepare Small chili (optional)
  8. Get Big chili
  9. You need Basil as garnish (optional)
Steps to make Pear stew pork belly:
  1. Prep all your ingredients as as seen below. Heat your wok and cooking oil,then fry garlic,tomato and pear until fragrance,and soft. About 10minutes.
  2. Then add in pork belly,goji berries and chili.Stir well and add in abit of water. Leave it for 15-20minutes low medium heat.
  3. Finally add salt.check if it is up to your liking then finally add in big chili and basil as garnish.stir stir for one two minutes then it is done.happy try

When the pork belly pieces start to cook, add the soy sauce. Take the pork out of the fridge and pat the skin dry. Put the rosemary leaves, salt and pepper in a mini chopper and grind together (or do this with a mortar and pestle). Rub the rosemary salt all over the pork, making sure it gets into the cuts in the skin. Return the pork and juices to the pan, season, then reduce to a simmer and cover.
