The ingredients needed to cook Salmon "Soup":
  1. You need 1 kg salmon (head, tail - included)
  2. Get 1 carrot
  3. You need 1 parsnip
  4. Take 1 red bell pepper
  5. Take 1 green bell pepper
  6. Provide 2 onions
  7. You need 3 celery sticks
  8. You need 1 bunch parsley
  9. Use 1 bunch lovage
  10. Use 1 lemon
  11. Use 1 tin mackerel fillets in tomato sauce
  12. Provide Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Salmon "Soup":
  1. Chop all vegetables finely and add them in a pan with 2 liters of water
  2. When all vegetables are half way cooked add the salmon and cook for another 10 min
  3. Add the mackerel tin with the tomato sauce including
  4. Squeeze the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper
  5. Add parsley and lovage for garnish

Take the soup kettle and boil the water. Put the noodles in a heatproof bowl and pour over boiled water from the kettle. This Finnish salmon soup uses cream as a base and is made of salmon chunks, potatoes, leeks and generously garnished with dill. It doesn't sound too fancy, but believe me, it has everything you need. I used heavy cream but you can substitute it with lighter one.
