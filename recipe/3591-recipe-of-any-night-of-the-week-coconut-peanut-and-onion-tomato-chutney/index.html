  4. You need 1/2 bowl Soaked Chana(gram) Dal
  5. Prepare 1 bowl Coconut
  6. You need 5 Green chillies
  7. Take 4 Whole Red Chillies
  8. You need 1 bowl Curd
  9. Use 2 tsp Tamarind pulp
  10. Provide 10 gm Ginger
  11. Provide 5-6 Garlic Cloves
  12. Prepare 2 tsp Chana dal
  13. Prepare 2 tsp Urad dal
  14. Get To taste Salt
  15. Prepare 1/2 tsp Asafoetida
  16. You need 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  17. Provide 15-20 Curry leaves
  18. Use 1 tsp Sugar
  19. You need 1 tsp Black Mustard seeds
  20. Provide For Onion Tomato Chutney
  21. You need 1 Onion roughly chopped
  22. Prepare 2 Tomatoes roughly chopped
  23. Take 3 tsp Oil
  24. Use 1/4 bowl Chana dal
  25. Use 1/4 Bowl Urad dal
  26. You need 5 gm Ginger
  27. Prepare 6-7 garlic cloves
  28. You need 15-20 Curry leaves
  29. Get 1/4 Tsp Asafoetida
  30. Prepare 1/2 tsp Black Mustard seeds
  31. You need 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  32. Provide 1/4 Bowl Tamarind pulp
  33. Provide 5 Whole Red Chillies
  34. Prepare to taste salt
  35. You need 1/2 bowl Water
Instructions to make Coconut Peanut and Onion Tomato Chutney:
  1. First Take the Peanut in the Pan and dry roast.then Cool down and remove the skin from the peanut.for Coconut Chutney take a Pan add oil 1 tsp.add Soaked Chana Dal and roast.then cools.
  2. Take a grinderjar add green chilli 2,ginger-5 gm, Coconut, roasted Chana Dal, roasted peanuts 1+1/2 tsp,curd,salt1tsp,sugar 1 tsp.grind well to make a paste.now add 1/2 of Small bowl water.mix well and grind again.
  3. For tadka take a Pan add Oil-2 tsp,add cumin seeds 1/2 tsp, black Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp, Asafoetida-1/4 tsp, whole Red Chilli-2,curry leaves and roast.then mix it in the Coconut paste.now very delicious and tasty Coconut Chutney is Ready.
  4. For Peanut Chutney take a Pan add 1 tsp of Oil,now add 1+1/2 tsp chana dal,1+1/2 tsp of urad dal and roast.now add green chilli 3, Ginger-5 gm, garlic-7/8 Cloves, Roasted Peanut -1/2 of Small bowl. Roast Well.then Cool the mixture.then put in grinderjar add water 1/2 of Small bowl, tamarind pulp.grind well.
  5. Now Peanut Paste is Ready.for tadka take a Pan add oil 2 tsp,now add black Mustard seeds-1/2 tsp, Cumin seeds-1/2tsp, Asafoetida-1/4 tsp, whole Red Chilli-2,curry leaves and roast well.put in Peanut Paste.
  6. Now very Delicious and tasty Peanut Chutney is Ready.
  7. For Onion Tomato Chutney take a Pan add oil-2 tsp,add Chana Dal-1/2 of Small bowl,urad dal-1/2 of Small bowl,garlic,ginger,whole Red Chilli 3, chopped onion.saute only.now add Chopped Tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.then Cool the mixture.put it in the grinderjar add salt 1 tsp, tamarind pulp and make a paste.
  8. Onion Tomato paste is Ready.now make tadka for that take a Pan add oil-2 tsp,add black Mustard seeds, Cumin seeds, Asafoetida, whole Red Chilli-2,curry leaves and roast well.put in the paste.now your very delicious and tasty Onion Tomato Chutney is Ready to serve.
  9. All the Chutney are Ready to serve.serve with any South indian Breakfast Recipe.thanks alot.
