The ingredients needed to prepare Spicy Chicken Tikka Pizza #CookpadRamadan iftarspecial with huma:
  1. Provide olive oil, for the tray
  2. Get 2 cups plain flour
  3. You need 7 g packet easy-blend dried yeast
  4. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  5. You need 150 ml warm water
  6. Provide 85 ml passata
  7. Prepare 100 g mozzarella, half grated, half chopped
  8. Use 2 tbsp grated parmesan or vegetarian alternative
  9. You need 1 red onions (small slices)
  10. Get 1 pepper (small slices)
  11. Get Jalapenos or bullet chilli
  12. Get Chilli and garlic oil (to put it on top)
  13. Get Oregano (to sprinkle)
  14. Get Chicken 1/2 kg boneless in small cubes (to make chicken tikka)
  15. Prepare Shan tikka masala packet (half)
  16. Use Ginger/garlic paste
  17. Prepare Oil (for chicken tikka)
Steps to make Spicy Chicken Tikka Pizza #CookpadRamadan iftarspecial with huma:
  1. Method - - Marinate chicken with tikka masala, ginger/garlic paste and little bit oil. Cook in oven or in a pot. Then make small pieces/shredded. Or you can cut small tiny pieces first then marinate then cook.
  2. Heat oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 9. Oil a 20 x 24cm baking tray. Mix the flour, yeast, ¼ tsp salt and the sugar in a bowl, then stir in the water. Bring the mixture together, knead until smooth, then press into the oiled tray.
  3. Spread passata over the dough, leaving a thin border round the edge. Season the passata, then topped with onion, pepper, chicken tikka, the mozzarella, the Parmesan and jalapenos/green chillies and sprinkle oregano on top bake for 15-20 mins or until cooked through. - Serve with a side salad.
