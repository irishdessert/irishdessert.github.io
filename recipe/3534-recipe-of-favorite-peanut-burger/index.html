The ingredients needed to cook Peanut burger:
  1. Get 4 tsp flour
  2. Provide 2 tsp milk
  3. You need 1 cup groundnut
  4. Use 1 tsp sugar
  5. Provide Pinch salt
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Provide Oil
Instructions to make Peanut burger:
  1. Wash the groundnut and remove the bad ones and dry
  2. In a bowl break the egg and mix with milk, sugar, salt and keep aside
  3. In a bowl put that groundnut than add the egg mixture and turn and put some flour and mix
  4. Keep repeating the process till the egg mixture finish
  5. In a pan put oil and allow to get hot than start frying
  6. Enjoy
