The ingredients needed to cook Doggy Treat Balls:
  1. Provide 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  2. Get 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  3. You need 1 carrot
  4. Provide 1 cup rolled oats
  5. Take 1/2 cup coconut oil
  6. Take 1/2 cup peanut butter
  7. Prepare Additional:
  8. Take 1/2 cup rolled oats
Instructions to make Doggy Treat Balls:
  1. Mix all the ingredients (except the additional 1/2 cup oats) in a food processor until well combined.
  2. Add the additional 1/2 cup and combine with a wooden spoon.
  3. Roll in tablespoon balls and refrigerate.
  4. The tablespoon ball size works perfect for medium sized dogs, smaller or larger breeds adjust the size as required.
