The ingredients needed to prepare Pinotage poached pear:
  1. Provide 6 pears, ripe, peeled
  2. Prepare 750 ml pinotage wine
  3. Prepare 150 ml brown sugar
  4. You need 1 lemon, zest and juice
  5. Provide 1 cinnamon stick
Instructions to make Pinotage poached pear:
  1. In a pot put all ingredrient except pears and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Peel pears but leave stems attached
  3. Place pears in liquid, close with lid and poach for 30-45 minutes on low heat **Turn occasionally to ensure colour spreads evenly
  4. Remove pot from heat and let cool till room temperature.
  5. Place pears in container with liquid to store in fridge for a minimum of 2 hours (can be made a day in advance) before service
