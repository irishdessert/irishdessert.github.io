The ingredients needed to make Homemade gnocchi with spinach pesto:
  1. Use 30 g cherry tomatoes
  2. Provide Gnocchi
  3. Use 1 kg potatoes
  4. Get 350 g flour
  5. Get 5 ml salt
  6. Get 2 ml nutmeg
  7. Prepare 2 eggs, large
  8. Use 80 g butter
  9. You need Spinach pesto
  10. Prepare 50 g baby spinach
  11. Prepare 20-30 ml olive oil
  12. Prepare 15 ml pine nuts
  13. Prepare 15 ml lemon juice
  14. You need 20 g parmesan cheese
  15. Prepare To taste salt
  16. Take To taste pepper
Steps to make Homemade gnocchi with spinach pesto:
  1. Bake potatoes in a roasting pan in a 180°C oven for 1 hour.
  2. Remove potatoes. Let cool slightly. Peel the skin off. Tip** season skins with some spices and serve as a snack.
  3. Mash potatoes. Gently mix in flour, eggs, salt and nutmeg to form a soft dough.
  4. Roll dough into a sausage form about 1.5cm thick. Cut about 1cm blocks.
  5. Use a fork and toll them from the bottom to the tip of the fork.
  6. Boil a pot of water. Add the pieces of gnocchi. As soon as they float remove from water and add to roasting pan. Add blocks of butter and grill in oven for few minutes.
  7. Pesto: In a blend add your baby spinach, olive oil, lemon juice, grated parmesan cheese, pine nuts, salt and pepper. Blend until desired consistency.
  8. Season gnocchi with salt and pepper. Serve with spinach pesto and some fresh cherry tomatoes.
