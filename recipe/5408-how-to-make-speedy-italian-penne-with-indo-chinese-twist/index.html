The ingredients needed to prepare Italian penne with Indo-Chinese  twist:
  1. Use For pasta
  2. Prepare 350 gm Penne pasta
  3. Get 1 tsp Salt
  4. Use 2 tbsp oil
  5. Provide For chicken
  6. Use 250 gm Chicken
  7. You need 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  8. You need 1 tsp Salt
  9. Get For veggies
  10. Get 1/2 cup grated cabbage
  11. Prepare 1 chopped capsicum
  12. Prepare For masala
  13. Get 1 tsp chopped ginger
  14. Take 1 tsp chopped garlic
  15. Take 1 tsp green chilli paste
  16. Provide 1 tbsp soya sauce
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp chilli sauce
  18. Prepare 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  19. Use 1 tsp black pepper powder
  20. Use 1 tsp Roasted cumin powder
  21. You need 2 whole green chilli
  22. Prepare If needed salt
  23. Prepare 2 tbsp Oil
  24. Get As needed Shredded cheese for topping
Steps to make Italian penne with Indo-Chinese  twist:
  1. In a pressure cooker and chicken, ginger garlic paste, Salt and cook till 2 whistles.
  2. Dry all water from chicken and shreds it
  3. Boil pasta in large pot with salt and 1 tbsp oil.
  4. When done take in seive and run under tap water. Again drop 1 tbsp oil and toss it.
  5. Heat oil in large pan or Kadai, add chopped ginger, chopped garlic, saute till light brown.
  6. Add whole red chilli, cabbage, capsicum and saute till soft.
  7. Add all spices,sauces and Salt and cook for 1 minute.
  8. Now add shredded chicken and 1/2 cup water and cook till oil separates.
  9. Now add boiled pasta and mix well.
  10. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  11. Serve with shredded cheese.
