The ingredients needed to make Chicken fettuccine pasta in white sauce:
  1. Get Fettuccine pasta
  2. Use Chicken breast
  3. You need 1 tbsp oil
  4. Take 1 seasoning cube
  5. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp cayyen pepper
  7. Use 1/2 tsp BBQ spices
  8. Provide White sauce ingredients
  9. Provide 1 tbsp flour
  10. Take 1 tbsp butter
  11. Use 1 cup milk
  12. Get 1/2 small onions (chopped)
  13. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  15. Provide Spinach (optional)
Steps to make Chicken fettuccine pasta in white sauce:
  1. These are the ingredients for this recipe
  2. Coat chicken breast with cayyen pepper,BBQ spices,black pepper and seasoning,leave it for at least 10mins
  3. Bring water to a boil, add some salt and oil then add the fettuccine pasta
  4. Cook fettuccine pasta for about 10-15mins or until cooked,drain and keep aside
  5. Heat up oil in a skillet and toast the chicken breast,this will take about 5 mins on each side at a medium low heat,until it is cooked through,then keep aside
  6. In the same pan,melt butter,and add flour stir for about 2mins then stir in the milk,stir quickly to avoid lumps
  7. Cook for couple of minutes the add,salt,black pepper,garlic, onions and spinach
  8. Lastly add in your cooked fettuccine pasta in the prepared white sauce and simmer for 1 to 2 and take the heat off!
  9. Serve and enjoy
