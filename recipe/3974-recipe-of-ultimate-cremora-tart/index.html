The ingredients needed to prepare Cremora tart:
  1. You need 1 cup cold water
  2. You need 2 cup Cremora coffee creamer
  3. Use 1/2 can evaporated milk
  4. You need 1/2 can condensed milk
  5. Take 3/4 cup lemon juice
  6. Provide 1 packages Jelly or jello any flavour or colour if you'd like
Steps to make Cremora tart:
  1. Make the jelly with the 1 cup of water
  2. Add coffee wither or cremora and mix very well with cake mixer.
  3. Mix in condensed and evaporated milk
  4. Add 3/4 cup lemon juice. Mix very well. It should become a bit stiff now.
  5. Place biscuits in flat bole and add mixture.
  6. Top it with what you like: fruit, chocolate, etc.
  7. Place in freezer till serve
