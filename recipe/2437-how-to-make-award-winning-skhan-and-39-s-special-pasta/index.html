The ingredients needed to make SKhan's special pasta.:
  1. Get 400 grams pasta
  2. Take 4 clove garlic cloves
  3. Use 6 tomato, chopped
  4. You need 1 tbsp Tomota paste
  5. Use salt
  6. You need 1 cup tomato ketchup
  7. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  8. You need 1 tsp chilli powder
  9. You need 1 medium capsicum, julian
  10. Get 2 cup sausages, julian
  11. Use 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp oregano
Instructions to make SKhan's special pasta.:
  1. Boil the pasta and keep aside with olive oil added.
  2. Fry the garlic until light brown then add the ingredients from 3-8 and mix it well for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the pasta and mix it.
  4. Add sausages and capsicum and mix. Add oregano.
  5. Plate it out and garnish it with mozzarella cheese.
