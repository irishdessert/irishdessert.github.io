The ingredients needed to cook Bake penne:
  1. Get 1 lb ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Take 2 cans tomatoes sauce
  4. Provide 1 bay leaf
  5. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  6. Get 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  7. You need 2 cups Colby- jack cheese
  8. Take to taste Salt and pepper
Steps to make Bake penne:

  2. Cook penne as according to package directions drain pasta and add to baking dish add 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese to pasta
  3. Add both can of tomatoes sauce to ground beef mixture and bay Leaf let simmer for 15 minutes
