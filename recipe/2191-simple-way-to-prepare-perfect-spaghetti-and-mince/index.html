The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti and mince:
  1. You need 1 kg Mince beef
  2. Take 4 cloves garlic
  3. Get Small ginger
  4. Prepare 1 onion
  5. Get 1 tsp paprika
  6. Use 1 tsp BBQ
  7. Use 1 tsp steak n chop
  8. You need To taste chillies flakes
  9. You need 1/4 tomato sauce
  10. You need 1 tbs sugar
  11. Use 3 tbs brown onion
  12. You need Olive oil
  13. Get Left over potatoes and carrots
  14. Get 1 cube beef stock
Instructions to make Spaghetti and mince:
  1. Chop the onion, garlic and grate the ginger.
  2. Pour the olive oil on the pot to then fry onion,garlic and ginger
  3. While it's cooking then pour the beef stock and season the mince with all the spices and sugar
  4. Stir the mince and then pour the tomato sauce
  5. While is cooked perfect then pour your last name night veggies
  6. As it has cooked then prepare your soup in cold water then pour it on your cooked mince
  7. Served with spaghetti
