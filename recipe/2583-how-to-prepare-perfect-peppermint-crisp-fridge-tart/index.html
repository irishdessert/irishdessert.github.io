The ingredients needed to make Peppermint crisp fridge tart:
  1. Prepare 2 packets tennis biscuits
  2. Get 500 ml fresh cream
  3. Provide 1 tin peppermint caramel treat
  4. Provide 1 slab of peppermint crisp chocolate (freeze over night)
Steps to make Peppermint crisp fridge tart:
  1. Beat cream until stif
  2. Add caramel treat and mix till smooth
  3. Layer tennis biscuits in square dish
  4. Pour half of the mixture over the biscuit and smooth out
  5. Crush the peppermint and throw half over
  6. Repeat the process♥♥♥

Pour the caramel into a bowl and mix it up with an electric hand mixer until it is smooth. This is an adaption of the traditional South African peppermint crisp tart recipe. Follow this easy recipeand create a dessert that's always a winner - in no time at all. A peppermint crisp tart is just what you need on a warm day to cool off. It contains layers of caramel, biscuit, peppermint crisp (mint chocolate), double cream and peppermint essence.
