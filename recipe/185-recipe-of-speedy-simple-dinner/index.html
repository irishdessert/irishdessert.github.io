The ingredients needed to make Simple dinner:
  1. You need 1 kg hake fish fillets
  2. Take 3 eggs
  3. Provide 1 cup flour
  4. Get 1 teaspoon paprika
  5. You need 1 teaspoon six gun grill spice
  6. Use 1/2 teaspoon aromat
  7. Provide 500 g mushrooms
  8. Use 1 onion, chopped
  9. You need 1 packet cream of mushroom powdered soup
  10. Get 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  11. Get 1/2 pepper, chopped
  12. Prepare 50 g margarine
  13. Use 3 tablespoons canola oil
  14. Take Pinch black pepper
  15. Prepare Sprinkle of aromat
Instructions to make Simple dinner:
  1. Thoroughly clean fish then in a bowl mix flour,paprika,six gun grill spice and aromat
  2. In another bowl beat eggs then heat on in a large pan,dip fish into eggs then into flour and fry until the fish is golden brown and crispy
  3. For mushroom gravy in a pot on a medium heat heat margarine then add onion and peppers then add mushrooms,aromat black pepper and thicken with cream of mushroom powdered soup
  4. Served with boiled rice
