The ingredients needed to make Raw banana peanut kofta in green gravy:
  1. You need For kofta
  2. Get 3 raw banana
  3. Provide 50 gram peanuts
  4. Use 1 spoon ginger chilli paste
  5. Prepare 1 spoon fennel seeds
  6. Use 1/2 spoon black pepper powder
  7. Use to taste Salt
  8. You need 1 spoon sugar
  9. Provide as needed Bread crumbs
  10. Take For green gravy
  11. Provide 1 bunch spinach
  12. Use 4-5 cashew nuts
  13. Use 1 spoon melon seeds
  14. Provide 4-5 almonds
  15. Prepare 1 spoon poppy seeds
  16. Take 1 chopped onion
  17. Take 2 tomato
  18. Prepare 2 Green chilli
  19. Get to taste Salt
  20. Take 1 spoon red chilli powder
  21. Take 1 spoon garam masala
  22. Get 2-3 spoon fresh cream
  23. Take 1 inch ginger
  24. Provide 1 spoon butter
  25. Provide 1 spoon oil
Instructions to make Raw banana peanut kofta in green gravy:
  1. To make a kofta, first boil the raw bananas with steam. Then smash it and add peanut powder, black pepper powder, fennel seeds, ginger chilli paste, salt, sugar, breadcrumbs to it. Make kofta and deepfry in hot oil.
  2. To make the green gravy, add the cinnamon, clove, cashew nuts, almonds, poppy seeds, melon seeds ginger, onion, green chillies and sprinkle some water in a cooker. Make only one whistle on slow flame. After that let them cool and mix in mixer. Grind the tomatoes in the mixer and make a smooth paste. Blanch the spinach and make a smooth paste in the mixer.
  3. Now take the butter and oil in a pan. Let it heat. Add the onion gravy made in the cooker. Saute for few minutes. When the oil is loose, add the tomato paste. Once the tomatoes are well mixed, add the spinach paste and allow to cook. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes. After the oil is loose, add salt, chilli, garam masala. Allow to cook for 2-3 minutes. Now turn off the gas and add the cream. Gravy is ready now.
  4. Now put the kofta in a dish and pour green gravy. Garnish with cheese, coriander and onions. serve with roti/paratha.
