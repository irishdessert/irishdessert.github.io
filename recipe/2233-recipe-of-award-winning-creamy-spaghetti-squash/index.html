The ingredients needed to prepare Creamy spaghetti squash:
  1. Prepare 1 medium winter squash
  2. Take 3 tablespoon cream cheese
  3. Get 1 tablespoon parsley
  4. Get 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. You need Salt and pepper
Steps to make Creamy spaghetti squash:
  1. Preheat oven to 180, on bake setting.Half the spaghetti squash, clean out seeds season with salt and pepper place on backing sheet and cook till soft
  2. Once cooked scope out the squash and set aside to cool off a bit
  3. Add cream cheese and parsley and season if you still need to. Enjoy
  4. This dish is great with a curry like in the picture, and salad.
