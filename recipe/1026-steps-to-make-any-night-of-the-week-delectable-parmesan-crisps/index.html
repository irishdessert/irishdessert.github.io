The ingredients needed to prepare Delectable Parmesan crisps:
  1. You need 5 oz Parmesan block
  2. Use 1 dash salt
  3. You need 1 dash basil (optional)
  4. Prepare 1 dash rosemary (optional)
  5. Prepare 1 dash garlic powder (optional)
  6. Provide 1 dash red pepper flakes (optional)
  7. You need 1 dash pepper (optional)
