The ingredients needed to make White Sauce Macaroni:
  1. Get For Cooking Macaroni
  2. Take 1 cup Macaroni
  3. Prepare 4 cup Water
  4. Get 1 tsp Salt
  5. Provide 1 tsp Oil
  6. Prepare For Veggies
  7. Take 1/4 cup chopped Capsicum
  8. You need 1/4 cup chopped Carrots
  9. Take 1 tbsp Butter
  10. Take 1 tbsp Red Chilli sauce
  11. Use to taste Salt
  12. Take For White Sauce
  13. You need 1 cup Milk
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp Butter
  15. Prepare 2 tbsp All Purpose flour
  16. You need 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  17. You need 1/4 tsp Black pepper powder
  18. Use 1 tsp Mix herbs
Instructions to make White Sauce Macaroni:
  1. Heat a saucepan and add butter.
  2. When the butter melts and starts to bubble, then add all purpose flour.
  3. Keep on stirring so that no lumps are formed and saut√© the flour till you get a nice cooked aroma from the flour and a pale golden colour.
  4. Pour 1 cup chilled milk with one hand and stir the sauce mixture with the other.
  5. Do stir frequently while the milk warms and heats up.
  6. On a low flame, add mayonnaise and stir the sauce till it thickens.
  7. Switch off the flame and keep the saucepan down and add the seasonings.
  8. Boil 4 cups water in a pan.
  9. When the water comes to a boil, add 1 cup macaroni pasta.
  10. Add salt and oil and cook the pasta on a medium to high flame.
  11. When the pasta gets done, switch off the flame and strain the pasta.
  12. In another pan heat 1 tbsp butter.
  13. Add chopped veggies, salt and red chilli sauce.
  14. Cook on high flame for 3-4 minutes.
  15. Then add them to the white sauce.
  16. Add boiled pasta and herbs and mix all well.
  17. Serve white sauce pasta garnished with some herbs.
