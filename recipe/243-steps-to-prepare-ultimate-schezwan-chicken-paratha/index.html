The ingredients needed to prepare Schezwan Chicken Paratha:
  1. Get 1 small onion chopped
  2. You need 2 green chilies finely chopped
  3. Prepare 1 tsp chopped garlic
  4. Take 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  5. Get 2 cups boiled & shredded chicken
  6. You need 1 cup noodles boiled
  7. Get 1/4 cup spring onion
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup coriander chopped
  9. Use 3-4 Tbsp Schezwan sauce
  10. Get 3 Tbsp tomato sauce
  11. Use 1 tsp chilli sauce
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp soya sauce
  13. Take 1 tsp vinegar
  14. Get to taste Salt
  15. You need Pinch lemon pepper
