The ingredients needed to make Pepper jelly:
  1. You need Finally chopped seeded green bell pepper 2 1/3 cup about two large peppers
  2. Get Finally chopped seeded DeVane jalapeño peppers about three small 1/3 cup
  3. You need 2/3 cup Cider vinegar
  4. Use Half a tablespoon of butter
  5. Take 3 1/3 cups sugar or 2 cups for reduced
  6. Provide 3 tablespoons Ball real fruit classic PECTIN
Instructions to make Pepper jelly:
  1. Using the freshtech jelly maker. Sprinkle PECTIN evenly over the bottom of pot fitted with stirrer. And add peppers evenly over PECTIN. Pour vinegar evenly over peppers add butter to help reduce foaming
  2. Press jelly button the cook time will automatically default to 25 minutes press the minus button until time reads 21 minutes press enter
  3. Wait four minutes for appliance to sound for short beeps that it is time to add the sugar and sugar gradually until empty place the lid on the pot
  4. The jelly maker peep when is done carefully remove the lid and the stirrer with a potholder, skin foam if there is any from top of jelly, preserve jelly immediately.
  5. See canning safety instructions from ball /blueball on how to can.
  7. Makes about 4 8ounce half pints of jelly. (how i used the pepper jelly, it went over the cream cheese)

What Kind of Peppers Should you Use for Pepper Jelly? For this Pepper Jelly recipe, I used a hodgepodge of sweet peppers harvested from my garden. There are red and green shishito peppers, yellow banana peppers, and cute little red and orange mad hatters.
