The ingredients needed to make Coffee pastry:
  1. Get 1 cup refined flour
  2. You need 1/2 cup melted butter
  3. Get 1/4 cup cocopowder
  4. Take 2 tbsp coffee
  5. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
  6. Use 1 cup milk
  7. Take 1/2 water
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  10. You need 1 cake tin
  11. Get 1 Pressure cooker
