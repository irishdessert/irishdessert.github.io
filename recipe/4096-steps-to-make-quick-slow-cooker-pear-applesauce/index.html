The ingredients needed to cook Slow cooker Pear Applesauce:
  1. Take 12 pears
  2. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar, sugar
  3. Provide 1 tsp salt, allspice, cinnamon, nutmeg
  4. Take 4 tablespoons orange juice
  5. Take 2 oz water
  6. You need 2 tsp vanilla
Steps to make Slow cooker Pear Applesauce:
  1. Peal, core, dice pears
  2. Place everything in crockpot
  3. Stir well
  4. Cook on low 4 to 6 hours, stirring occasionally
  5. When pears are cooked Smash with a potatoe masher til applesauce like or blend smoothe
  6. Serve or cool and serve

Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for six hours. To make a smooth sauce, puree the fruit using an immersion blender. For a chunkier sauce, just mash it with a potato masher. Serve warm or cool to room temperature and refrigerate for up to two weeks. Pour water or apple juice into the bottom of a large crockpot (slow cooker) and add the apple mixture.
